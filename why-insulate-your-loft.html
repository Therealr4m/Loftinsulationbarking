Why You Should Insulate Your Loft

Here are the simple reasons Barking homeowners choose insulation: energy savings, comfort and long-term protection.

Top reasons to insulate

  1. Prevent heat loss: up to 25% of home heat is lost through an uninsulated loft.
  2. Lower bills: insulation typically pays back within 2–4 years through energy savings.
  3. Protect your home: reduces condensation and damp, protecting roof timber.
  4. Comfort: reduces drafts and evens upstairs temperatures.
