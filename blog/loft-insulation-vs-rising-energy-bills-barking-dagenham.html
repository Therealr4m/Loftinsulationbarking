The Cost of Heat Loss Through the Roof (Localised Stats, Barking & Dagenham)

  • A poorly insulated loft can add £280–£450+ per year in wasted heating costs.
  • Homes with insulation below 150mm tend to lose heat 30–50% faster, and that forces your boiler to run much longer than usual and more frequently.
So, for every £1 spent on heating, a poorly insulated loft could potentially be leaking 40–60p straight into the sky. By comparison, upgrading to modern 270mm insulation can reduce heat loss by up to 25%.
