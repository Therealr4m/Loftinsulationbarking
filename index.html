Loft Insulation FAQs

How long does loft insulation take?

Most installations take between 2–4 hours for loose-fill or blown methods depending on your loft size. For traditional roll-out blanket insulation it can take around one day. If your're having both insualtion and boarding installed expect up to 3 days.

How much money can I save per year?

Most Barking & Dagenham homeowners have reported that they've saved around £300–£500 per year on heating bills after having insulation installed.

Do you remove old insulation?

Yes. We safely remove old, damp, dirty or ineffective insulation before installing new Rockwool or fiberglass insulation.

Do you cover all loft types?

Yes. We work on terraced, semi-detached, detached houses and bungalows.
