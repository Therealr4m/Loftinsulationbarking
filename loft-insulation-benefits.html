Loft Insulation Benefits — Save Energy & Money

Loft insulation is one of the quickest ways Barking & Dagenham homeowners reduce their heating bills, increase comfort and add value to their home. Below is what you can expect after a quality install by our local loft Insulation team :

1. Cut your heating bills

Having well fitted loft insulation will drop your energy costs significantly, the average Barking household saves £300–£500 per year after having proper loft insulation installed, energy prices vary, but insulation provides a predictable, long-term saving for homeowners.

2. Improved comfort all year

Top quality insulation means warm in winter and cooler in summer — Good insulation stabilises temperatures throughout your entire home and removes cold drafts from upstairs rooms.

3. Reduced noise

Mineral wool and thicker insulation types also provide acoustic benefits, cutting rooftop and external noise.

4. Longer life for your home

Good insulation helps control condensation and damp, therefore protecting your roof against roof timbers thereby extending the lifespan of your property.

5. Potentially increases property value

Better energy performance and an improved EPC rating makes homes more attractive to buyers and renters, also potentially meaning you can expect to rent out or sell your property on the higher range of the market.
