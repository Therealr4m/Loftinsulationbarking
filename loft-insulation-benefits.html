Loft Insulation Benefits — Save Energy & Money

Loft insulation is one of the quickest ways Barking homeowners reduce their heating bills, increase comfort and add value to their home. Below is what you can expect after a quality install:

1. Cut your heating bills

The average Barking household saves £300–£500 per year after proper loft insulation, energy prices vary, but insulation provides a predictable, long-term saving.

2. Improved comfort all year

Warm in winter and cooler in summer — Good insulation stabilises temperatures and removes cold drafts from upstairs rooms.

3. Reduced noise

Mineral wool and thicker insulation types also provide acoustic benefits, cutting rooftop and external noise.

4. Longer life for your home

Good insulation helps control condensation and damp, protecting roof timbers and extending the lifespan of the property.

5. Potentially increases property value

Better energy performance and an improved EPC rating makes homes more attractive to buyers and renters.
